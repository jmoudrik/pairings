Tournament Pairing Result Forms – v0.2

This tool generates Result Forms for Go Tournaments. The winning player can encircle his victory, sign (both players) and put the form into a result box. The purpose of this is to simplify tournaments – the usual method of encircling on the pairing paper gets impractical for large tournaments.

Have a look on an Example Result Form.
The tool was used during EGC 2015, or Moravian Open Brno 2015.

The Tool


  • Currently, only the txt export of Christhoph Gerlach's MacMahon pairing application is supported. See the Example export file.
